================================================================================
NOMINAL OPERATIONS PROCEDURE - 6U CubeSat Mission
================================================================================
Research Subject Reference - Follow steps in order during nominal experiments

================================================================================
SECTION 1: COMMUNICATIONS SETUP
================================================================================

STEP 1.1: Establish Downlink
Command: COMM_TX_RX
Verify: Link_Status = LOCK

STEP 1.2: Verify Flight Computer
Verify: CDH is ON and uptime incrementing
Verify: CDH boot_count stable
Verify: No CDH faults

================================================================================
SECTION 2: ATTITUDE CONTROL INITIALIZATION
================================================================================

STEP 2.1: Enable ADCS
Command: ADCS_ON
Verify: ADCS mode = DETUMBLE

STEP 2.2: Wait for Detumble Completion
Wait: Until roll, pitch, and yaw rates reach 0 ± 0.1 deg/s
Verify: Angular rates stabilized
Verify: ADCS enabled = True

================================================================================
SECTION 3: SOLAR ARRAY DEPLOYMENT
================================================================================

STEP 3.1: Deploy Solar Array A
Command: EPS_DEPLOY_ARRAY_A
Wait: 10 seconds
Verify: Solar Array A status = DEPLOYED
Verify: Array A voltage > 0V
Verify: Array A current increasing

STEP 3.2: Deploy Solar Array B
Command: EPS_DEPLOY_ARRAY_B
Wait: 10 seconds
Verify: Solar Array B status = DEPLOYED
Verify: Array B voltage > 0V
Verify: Array B current increasing

================================================================================
SECTION 4: THERMAL CONTROL DEPLOYMENT
================================================================================

STEP 4.1: Deploy Radiator
Command: TCS_DEPLOY_RADIATOR
Wait: 10 seconds
Verify: Radiator status = DEPLOYED

STEP 4.2: Verify Thermal Stability
Verify: AVI CPU temperature stable
Verify: No thermal warnings
Verify: Temperature within nominal range (20-40°C)

================================================================================
SECTION 5: NAVIGATION SENSOR INITIALIZATION
================================================================================

STEP 5.1: Enable Navigation Sensors
Command: GPS_ON
Command: STAR_TRACKER_ON
Verify: GPS enabled = True
Verify: Star Tracker enabled = True

STEP 5.2: Wait for Star Tracker Acquisition
Wait: 180 seconds (3 minutes)
Verify: Star Tracker attitude_valid = True
Verify: Star Tracker attitude error < 3°

STEP 5.3: Wait for GPS Lock
Wait: 180 seconds (3 minutes) while GPS acquires satellites
Verify: GPS satellites ≥ 5
Verify: GPS position_valid = True
Verify: GPS PDOP < 40 (good position accuracy)

================================================================================
SECTION 6: SUN POINTING MODE
================================================================================

STEP 6.1: Command Sun Pointing
Command: ADCS_SUN_POINT
Verify: ADCS mode = SUN_POINT

STEP 6.2: Wait for Sun Acquisition
Wait: Until sun_angle approaches 0°
Verify: Sun angle < 5°
Verify: Solar power increasing
Verify: Battery A and B charging

================================================================================
SECTION 7: DESATURATION MANEUVER PREPARATION
================================================================================

STEP 7.1: Enable Propulsion System (5V Standby)
Command: PROP_STANDBY
Verify: PROP standby_mode = True
Verify: PROP telemetry flowing (not stale)
Verify: Tank pressure nominal (~20 bar)

STEP 7.2: Activate Propellant Tank Heater
Command: TCS_HEATER_PROP_ON
Verify: TCS heater_prop_on = True
Verify: Tank temperature rising

STEP 7.3: Enable Full Propulsion Power (VBat)
Command: PROP_ON
Verify: PROP voltage = 28V
Verify: PROP current increasing

STEP 7.4: Activate Catalyst Bed Heater
Command: PROP_HEATER_ON
Verify: PROP catalyst_heater_on = True
Verify: Catalyst temperature rising

STEP 7.5: Verify Power Budget and Battery Status
Verify: EPS total_power_in > total_power_out (positive power)
Verify: Battery A and B SOC stable or increasing (charging)
Verify: Battery A SOC > 25% (enough capacity to continue)
Verify: Battery B SOC > 25% (enough capacity to continue)
Verify: No EPS warnings

STEP 7.6: Wait for Catalyst Bed Ready
Wait: Until catalyst_temp > 300°C
Verify: Catalyst bed at operating temperature
Note: This ensures efficient propellant decomposition

================================================================================
SECTION 8: REACTION WHEEL DESATURATION
================================================================================

STEP 8.1: Command Desaturation Maneuver
Command: ADCS_DESAT
Verify: ADCS mode = DESAT
Verify: PROP valve_status = OPEN (propulsion active)

STEP 8.2: Monitor Desaturation Progress
Verify: Delta-V increasing (momentum being transferred)
Verify: Propellant mass decreasing
Verify: Reaction wheel RPMs decreasing (all 3 wheels)
Wait: Until wheel speeds < 1000 RPM

STEP 8.3: Complete Desaturation
Verify: Wheels desaturated
Verify: PROP valve_status = CLOSED (automatic)
Verify: ADCS returns to previous mode

================================================================================
SECTION 9: ORBIT RAISE MANEUVER PREPARATION
================================================================================

STEP 9.1: Command Target Attitude
Command: ADCS_GO_TO 50 -50 100
Note: Roll=50°, Pitch=-50°, Yaw=100° (thrust vector alignment)

STEP 9.2: Monitor Attitude Slew
Verify: Yaw, pitch, roll rates ≠ 0 (actively slewing)
Verify: Yaw, pitch, roll angles moving toward target
Verify: Reaction wheel RPMs changing (active control)

STEP 9.3: Verify Power Budget During Slew
Verify: EPS total_power_in > total_power_out
Verify: Battery A SOC > 25% (sufficient capacity for maneuver)
Verify: Battery B SOC > 25% (sufficient capacity for maneuver)
Verify: No power warnings

STEP 9.4: Wait for Attitude Acquisition
Wait: Until attitude reached (rates ~ 0, angles at target ±1°)
Verify: Roll = 50° ± 1°
Verify: Pitch = -50° ± 1°
Verify: Yaw = 100° ± 1°

STEP 9.5: Pre-Burn Checks
Verify: PROP catalyst_temp > 300°C
Verify: PROP standby_mode = True (system ready)
Verify: PROP valve_status = CLOSED
Verify: EPS total_power positive or battery SOC > 25%
Verify: Current orbital altitude ~ 520 km

================================================================================
SECTION 10: ORBIT RAISE BURN EXECUTION
================================================================================

STEP 10.1: Open Loop Thrust Firing
Command: PROP_VALVE_OPEN
Verify: Valve status = OPEN
Verify: Propellant mass decreasing
Verify: Delta-V increasing

STEP 10.2: Monitor Burn Progress
Watch: Propellant mass decreasing
Target: Continue until propellant_mass = 0.4 kg
Verify: Orbital altitude increasing
Verify: ADCS maintaining attitude

STEP 10.3: Terminate Burn at Target Mass
When: Propellant mass reaches 0.4 kg
Command: PROP_VALVE_CLOSE
Verify: Valve status = CLOSED
Verify: Propellant mass stopped decreasing

STEP 10.4: Verify Orbit Change
Verify: Orbital altitude changed from ~520 km
Verify: Orbital velocity changed
Note: Record new altitude for mission log

================================================================================
SECTION 11: POST-BURN RECOVERY
================================================================================

STEP 11.1: Return to Sun Pointing
Command: ADCS_SUN_POINT
Verify: ADCS mode = SUN_POINT
Verify: Spacecraft reorienting to sun
Wait: Until sun_angle < 5°

================================================================================
SECTION 12: PROPULSION SYSTEM SHUTDOWN
================================================================================

STEP 12.1: Ensure Valve Closed
Command: PROP_VALVE_CLOSE
Verify: Valve status = CLOSED (redundant safety check)

STEP 12.2: Disable Catalyst Bed Heater
Command: PROP_HEATER_OFF
Verify: PROP catalyst_heater_on = False

STEP 12.3: Disable Propellant Tank Heater
Command: TCS_HEATER_PROP_OFF
Verify: TCS heater_prop_on = False

STEP 12.4: Power Down Propulsion System
Command: PROP_OFF
Verify: PROP standby_mode = False
Verify: PROP telemetry goes stale
Verify: System fully shut down

================================================================================
SECTION 13: FINAL VERIFICATION
================================================================================

STEP 13.1: Verify Spacecraft State
Verify: ADCS in SUN_POINT mode
Verify: Solar arrays deployed and charging
Verify: Radiator deployed
Verify: GPS and Star Tracker operational
Verify: COMM in TX_RX mode
Verify: PROP system safely shut down
Verify: No warnings or cautions active
Verify: EPS power positive

STEP 13.2: Mission Complete
Note: Nominal operations procedure complete
Note: Spacecraft in stable power-positive configuration
Note: Ready for science operations or standby

================================================================================
END OF NOMINAL PROCEDURE
================================================================================

Estimated Completion Time: 15-20 minutes (depends on sensor acquisition times)
Critical Steps: Catalyst bed warmup (Step 7.6), Attitude acquisition (Step 9.4)
Safety Notes: Always verify power positive before major maneuvers
             Always verify catalyst temp before propulsion operations
             Monitor propellant mass during burns
